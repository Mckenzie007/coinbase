Help Topics

Account Setup

Coinbase makes account setup simple. Create your account in minutes with just an email and password. Our platform guides you through identity verification to ensure security and compliance.

Once verified, you can start trading cryptocurrencies with ease.

Buying & Selling

Buying and selling cryptocurrencies on Coinbase is straightforward. Our intuitive interface allows you to trade with various payment methods including bank transfers and debit cards.

Real-time market data helps you make informed trading decisions.

Security

Coinbase employs industry-leading security measures to protect your assets. We store 98% of customer funds offline in cold storage and use AES-256 encryption for all data.

Two-factor authentication adds an extra layer of security to your account.

Transactions

Send and receive cryptocurrencies quickly with Coinbase. Our platform provides clear transaction details including fees and estimated completion times.

Track your transaction history with detailed records for all your crypto activities.
